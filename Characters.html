Tanjiro kamado

Tanjiro Kamado is the main protagonist of *Demon Slayer* who embarks on a journey to become a Demon Slayer and avenge his family, who were slaughtered by demons. Tanjiro is known for his unwavering kindness, determination, and strong sense of justice. Despite the tragic events in his life, his love for his younger sister Nezuko, who was turned into a demon, drives him to fight against the forces of darkness. His skills with the Water Breathing techniques and his ability to master the Hinokami Kagura (Dance of the Fire God) make him a formidable swordsman.

  • Age: 15
  • Abilities: Water Breathing, Hinokami Kagura, Enhanced Strength
  • Affiliation: Demon Slayer Corps

Zenitsu Agatsuma

Zenitsu Agatsuma is a member of the Demon Slayer Corps and a close friend of Tanjiro Kamado. Though often portrayed as fearful and cowardly, Zenitsu reveals immense courage when his friends are in danger. He is a master of the Thunder Breathing techniques, especially his signature technique, the Thunderclap and Flash, which he can use with incredible speed and precision. Zenitsu is known for his loyalty and his desire to protect his friends, despite his outwardly nervous personality.

  • Age: 16
  • Abilities: Thunder Breathing, Thunderclap and Flash
  • Affiliation: Demon Slayer Corps

Inosuke Hashibira

Inosuke Hashibira is a wild and brash member of the Demon Slayer Corps, known for his boar-headed appearance and his unique fighting style. Raised by wild boars in the mountains, Inosuke is initially rough around the edges but shows deep loyalty and care for his friends. He uses the Beast Breathing techniques, which he created by adapting his knowledge of animal instincts. Despite his aggressive nature, Inosuke has a strong sense of honor and growth as he travels with Tanjiro and Zenitsu to defeat demons and protect humanity.

  • Age: 15
  • Abilities: Beast Breathing, Superhuman Strength
  • Affiliation: Demon Slayer Corps

Nezuko kamado

Nezuko Kamado is the younger sister of Tanjiro Kamado and one of the central characters in *Demon Slayer*. After her family is killed by demons and she is turned into one, Nezuko retains her human emotions and fights to protect her brother and others. Despite her transformation, she has incredible strength and is a skilled fighter, using her enhanced demon abilities alongside her love for her family. Throughout the series, Nezuko’s bond with Tanjiro becomes a driving force in their mission to find a cure for her demon curse.

  • Age: 14 (before transformation)
  • Abilities: Enhanced Strength, Blood Demon Art, Regeneration
  • Affiliation: Demon Slayer Corps (indirectly)

Giyu tomioka

Giyu Tomioka is the Water Hashira of the Demon Slayer Corps, known for his calm and stoic personality. A highly skilled swordsman, Giyu uses the Water Breathing techniques to slay demons with precision and speed. He is the first to encounter Tanjiro and Nezuko Kamado and plays a pivotal role in their journey. Despite his cold demeanor, Giyu has a strong sense of justice and is deeply committed to his duty as a Hashira. His strict adherence to the Code of the Demon Slayer Corps makes him a reliable, though sometimes distant, ally.

  • Age: 21
  • Abilities: Water Breathing, Unyielding Resolve
  • Affiliation: Demon Slayer Corps (Water Hashira)

Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto Uzumaki is a spirited ninja with a dream of becoming the Hokage of his village. Host to the Nine-Tails Fox, he is known for his determination, loyalty, and boundless energy. His journey is a tale of resilience and triumph.

  • Age: 17
  • Abilities: Rasengan, Shadow Clone Jutsu
  • Affiliation: Hidden Leaf Village

Sasuke Uchiha

Sasuke Uchiha is a prodigious ninja and one of the last surviving members of the Uchiha clan. Driven by the desire to avenge his family, he embarks on a path of power and revenge. His mastery of the Sharingan and determination define him as one of the strongest ninjas of his generation.

  • Age: 17
  • Abilities: Chidori, Sharingan, Amaterasu
  • Affiliation: Hidden Leaf Village (formerly), Rogue Ninja

Itachi Uchiha

Itachi Uchiha was a genius ninja from the Uchiha clan, known for his tragic past and immense strength. A member of the Akatsuki organization, he is infamous for his tragic role in the massacre of the Uchiha clan, an act that he carried out under orders to protect his village. His Sharingan abilities, particularly the Tsukuyomi and Amaterasu, made him one of the most formidable opponents in the Naruto universe.

  • Age: 21 (at the time of death)
  • Abilities: Sharingan, Tsukuyomi, Amaterasu, Susanoo
  • Affiliation: Uchiha Clan, Akatsuki

Madara Uchiha

Madara Uchiha was one of the founding members of the Hidden Leaf Village and the leader of the Uchiha clan. Known for his ruthless ambition, Madara sought ultimate power, which he achieved by mastering the Sharingan and later the Rinnegan. His rivalry with Hashirama Senju and his desire to cast the world into a dream are central to his story, as he becomes one of the most formidable antagonists in the *Naruto* series.

  • Age: 90+ (Resurrected)
  • Abilities: Sharingan, Rinnegan, Perfect Susanoo, Wood Style
  • Affiliation: Uchiha Clan, Akatsuki (formerly)

Monkey D. Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy is the ambitious and carefree captain of the Straw Hat Pirates in *One Piece*. With the goal of becoming the Pirate King, Luffy is known for his resilience, loyalty to his friends, and determination. His Devil Fruit power, the Gum-Gum Fruit, grants him the ability to stretch his body like rubber, making him an incredibly formidable fighter. Throughout his journey, Luffy gathers a diverse crew, forms lasting friendships, and takes on powerful adversaries to achieve his dream.

  • Age: 19
  • Abilities: Gum-Gum Fruit (Gomu Gomu no Mi), Haki
  • Affiliation: Straw Hat Pirates

Light Yagami

Light Yagami is a brilliant but morally conflicted high school student who stumbles upon a mysterious notebook known as the Death Note. With the power to kill anyone whose name he writes in it, Light decides to rid the world of criminals and create a utopia under his rule. However, his actions attract the attention of the genius detective L, sparking a thrilling cat-and-mouse game between the two. Light’s descent into madness and his complex view of justice make him one of the most intriguing antiheroes in anime.

  • Age: 17
  • Abilities: Genius intellect, Death Note
  • Affiliation: None (formerly, Kira)

Hinata and Kageyama

Hinata Shoyo is a passionate and determined volleyball player with an unstoppable will to improve. Despite his short stature, his incredible jumping ability and boundless energy make him a formidable opponent. Alongside him is Kageyama Tobio, a genius setter with exceptional skills and a strong, sometimes harsh personality. Initially rivals, their relationship grows into a powerful partnership as they learn to trust and complement each other on the court, forming the perfect setter-spiker duo for the Karasuno High volleyball team.

  • Hinata Shoyo: Age: 16, Abilities: Super Jump, Quick Attack, Affiliation: Karasuno High
  • Kageyama Tobio: Age: 16, Abilities: Perfect Sets, Strong Serve, Affiliation: Karasuno High

Gojo Satoru

Satoru Gojo is one of the most powerful sorcerers in the *Jujutsu Kaisen* universe. As a teacher at the Tokyo Metropolitan Magic Technical College, he is known for his immense strength and confidence in his abilities. Gojo possesses the Six Eyes and the Limitless Cursed Technique, which grant him near godlike powers. His carefree, sometimes cocky attitude contrasts with his dedication to protect his students and ensure the future of the jujutsu world. Gojo's unmatched combat abilities and charisma make him an iconic character in the series.

  • Age: 28
  • Abilities: Six Eyes, Limitless Cursed Technique, Infinity
  • Affiliation: Jujutsu High
